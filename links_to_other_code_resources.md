# Links to Manuscript resources

This section refers to already published manuscripts for which codes are publicly available. They are classified in chronological order.

## October 2021: Mutographs ESCC Manuscript

* **When:** October 2021
* **Where:** Nature Genetics (<https://www.nature.com/articles/s41588-021-00928-6>)
* **What:** Analysis of 552 genomes (WGS) from esophageal squamous cell carcinoma patients
The downstream analyses codes are available at <https://gitlab.com/Mutographs/Mutographs_ESCC>

### Code authors: 
* Signature extractions and attributions: *Sarah Moody* (Sanger), *Sergey Senkin* (IARC)
* Epidemiological associations: *Sergey Senkin* (IARC)
* Driver genes: *Sarah Moody* (Sanger)
* Germline annotations and analysis (ancestry, PRS): *Joshua Atkins* (IARC), *Ricardo Cortez Cardoso Penha* (IARC)

## May 2024: Mutographs RCC Manuscript

* **When:** May 2024
* **Where:** Nature (<https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-024-07368-2>)
* **What:** Analysis of 962 genomes (WGS) from clear cell renal cell carcinoma patients.
The downstream analyses codes are available at <https://gitlab.com/Mutographs/Mutographs_RCC>

### Code authors:
* Signature extractions and attributions: *Sarah Moody* (Sanger), *Sergey Senkin* (IARC)
* Epidemiological associations: *Sergey Senkin* (IARC)
* Driver genes: *Sarah Moody* (Sanger)
* Geospatial analysis: *Thomas Cattiaux*
* Metabolomics analysis: *Thomas Cattiaux* 
* Germline annotations and analysis: *Joshua Atkins* (IARC), *Ricardo Cortez Cardoso Penha* (IARC)
* Evolutionary analysis: *Marcos Diaz-Gay* (UCSD), *Mariya Kazachkova* (UCSD)

## March 2025: Mutographs HNC Manuscript

* **When:** March 2025
* **Where:** Nature Genetics (<https://www.nature.com/articles/s41588-025-02134-0>)
* **What:** Analysis of 265 genomes (WGS) from head and neck cancer patients.
The downstream analyses codes are available at GitLab (<https://gitlab.com/Mutographs/mutographs-hnc>) and Zenodo (<https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.14851388>).

### Code authors:
* Signature extractions and attributions: *Sarah Moody* (Sanger), *Laura Torrens* (IARC)
* Epidemiological associations: *Laura Torrens* (IARC)
* Driver genes: *Sarah Moody* (Sanger)
* Germline annotations and analysis: *Laura Torrens* (IARC), *Ricardo Cortez Cardoso Penha* (IARC)

## April 2025: Mutographs CRC Manuscript

* **When:** April 2025
* **Where:** Nature (<https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-025-09025-8>)
* **What:** Analysis of 265 genomes (WGS) from head and neck cancer patients.
981 genomes from colon and rectal adenocarcinoma patients. Analyses were conducted separately for DNA mismatch repair-proficient cases (MSS) and DNA mismatch repair-deficient cases (MSI). The downstream analyses code are available at <https://github.com/AlexandrovLab/Mutographs_CRC>.

### Code authors:
* Signature extractions and attributions: *Sarah Moody* (Sanger), *Marcos Diaz-Gay* (UCSD)
* Epidemiological associations: *Wellington dos Santos* (IARC)
* Driver genes: *Sarah Moody* (Sanger)
* Evolutionary analys: *Marcos Diaz-Gay* (UCSD), *Mariya Kazachkova* (UCSD)
* Germline annotations and analysis: *Wellington dos Santos* (IARC), *Ricardo Cortez Cardoso Penha* (IARC)
